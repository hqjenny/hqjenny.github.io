Qijing Jenny Huang is a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley, advised by Prof. John Wawrzynek. Her interests are in computer architecture, computer-aided design, reconfigurable computing, and machine learning. She has been working on building efficient FPGA accelerators for emerging ML applications, HLS-based hardware/software flow, and ML-assisted HLS and compiler transformation. Her thesis work focuses on novel design and scheduling techniques for accelerating machine learning algorithms on heterogeneous spatial architecture.
