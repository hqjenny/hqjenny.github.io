Electrical Engineering and Computer Sciences Qijing Jenny Huang is a PhD student in Computer Science at the University of California, Berkeley , advised by Prof. John Wawrzynek. Her interests are in computer architecture, computer-aided design, reconfigurable computing, and machine learning. She has been working on building efficient FPGA accelerators for emerging ML applications, HLS-based hardware/software flow, and ML-assisted HLS and compiler transformation. Her thesis work focuses on novel design and scheduling techniques for accelerating machine learning algorithms on heterogeneous spatial architecture. She received her B.A.Sc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Toronto, where she was granted the University of Toronto Excellence Awards for her undergraduate research.
